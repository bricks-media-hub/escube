What is HVAC Systems?

HVAC stands for Heating, Ventilating, and Air Conditioning. It covers everything from home air conditioners to large industrial systems. A well-designed HVAC setup ensures thermal control and indoor comfort using principles of thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and heat transfer.

You'll often see big AC boxes on rooftops of buildings—these are part of HVAC systems. They're vital in industrial complexes, skyscrapers, and regulated environments where temperature and humidity must be controlled.

  • Heating systems for colder climates
  • Ventilation for air quality control
  • Air conditioning for temperature regulation
  • Custom solutions for various environments
